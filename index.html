FAQ

Common questions.

Do I need a doctor's prescription to subscribe? +
No prescription needed. You simply tell us your condition during sign-up, and our dietitians design a plan based on clinical guidelines for that condition. If you have an existing diet chart from your doctor, you can share it and we'll match it exactly.
Where do you currently deliver? +
We currently deliver in Noida Sectors 18, 50, and 62, and parts of Greater Noida. We're expanding across the NCR rapidly — enter your area during sign-up and we'll notify you when we reach you.
Can I customise meals if I have allergies? +
Absolutely. During onboarding you'll specify allergies, intolerances, and food dislikes. Our dietitians build these into your plan. Clinical Plus subscribers can also request specific modifications via WhatsApp.
Can I pause or cancel my subscription? +
Yes, you can pause for up to 7 days per month (e.g., when travelling) with 24 hours' notice. Cancellations can be made anytime before your next billing cycle with no penalty.
How fresh are the meals? +
Every meal is cooked on the day of delivery in our FSSAI-certified kitchen. We do not use frozen or pre-cooked meals. Packaging is tamper-proof and includes cook time, macros, and a best-by window.
Will I see actual health improvements? +
Dietary compliance is one of the most powerful levers in managing PCOD and Type 2 Diabetes. Customers on our Full Day and Clinical Plus plans who maintain consistency typically see measurable improvements in 6–12 weeks. Clinical Plus includes monthly health reports to track progress.
